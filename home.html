The Directorate of Tourism and Traditional Industry has created this site specifically for the various actors in the tourism sector with the aim of promoting and facilitating the public services provided to them.


Our Services


Create new tourism and travel agency

Unlock endless business potential with a travel agency that turns wanderlust into profit. Tap into a booming industry and start creating unforgettable journeys for eager travelers today!

Create a new Branch for your Agency

Expand your reach and boost profits by opening a new branch in a high-demand location. Capture more travelers, increase visibility, and grow your agency's presence in the booming travel market!

change your Agency Adresss

Unlock new opportunities with a strategic move! Changing your agency's address can increase foot traffic, enhance brand visibility, and attract a fresh wave of clients, driving your business to new heights.

Authorization to Operate a New Hotel Establishment

Secure your authorization to operate a new hotel establishment and take the first step toward success. With the right approvals, you can offer premium accommodations, elevate guest experiences, and build a thriving hospitality business.

Approval as Manager of a Hotel Establishment

Gain approval as the manager of a hotel establishment and unlock the potential to lead with excellence. With this authorization, you'll drive guest satisfaction, oversee daily operations, and shape the future success of your hotel.

Request for classification or reclassification of a hotel establishment

Ensure your hotel meets industry standards by requesting classification or reclassification. This process enhances your establishment's credibility, improves marketability, and ensures you attract the right clientele with the appropriate star rating.

Application for Approval as a Tourist Guide

Apply for approval as a certified tourist guide and become an ambassador of unforgettable experiences. With this certification, you'll gain credibility, enhance your expertise, and lead travelers on enriching journeys through unique destinations
